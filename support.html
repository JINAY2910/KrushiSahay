📞 Support & Helpline Directory

Below is a list of official government helplines and support channels to assist you with schemes, applications, or website-related queries. Use these contacts for accurate and verified assistance.

📌 Agriculture General Support (કૃષિ સહાય માટે)

Helpline: 1800-180-1551

Email: agri-help@gov.in

Hours: Mon–Fri, 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM

This is the main support helpline for general agricultural questions including seeds, fertilizers, farming techniques, and scheme awareness. Keep your location and query details ready when calling.

📌 PM-KISAN Helpline (પ્રધાનમંત્રી કિસાન યોજના)

Helpline: 155261 / 011-24300606

Email: pmkisan-ict@gov.in

Hours: Mon–Sat, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM

This support is for payment delays, Aadhaar mismatch, beneficiary status, and DBT-related queries. Have your Aadhaar, registered mobile, and PM-KISAN ID when you call.

📌 eNAM Market Support (ઇ-નામ – બજાર મદદ)

Helpline: 1800-270-0224

Email: support@enam.gov.in

Hours: Mon–Fri, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Get help registering as a farmer, listing your crops, resolving payment issues, and accessing APMC markets digitally through the National Agriculture Market portal.

📌 Kisan Call Center (કિસાન કોલ સેન્ટર)

Helpline: 1800-180-1551 (24x7)

Languages: Hindi, Gujarati, English, and more

Ask any agricultural question—crops, climate impact, pests, fertilizers. Calls are connected to agri experts or scientists based on your location and language.

📌 Soil Health Card & Schemes (માટી આરોગ્ય કાર્ડ)

Helpline: 1800-180-1111

Email: soilcard@gov.in

Support for farmers needing soil testing, retrieving lost cards, or understanding fertilizer recommendations based on their soil type. Share your card number and farm location when contacting.

📌 Technical / Portal Support (ટેકનિકલ સહાય)

Helpline: 1800-121-3121

Email: techsupport@krushisahay.gov.in

For website login issues, broken links, file upload errors, or slow portals. If you're stuck during online application, this is the right support line.

📌 WhatsApp Support

Number: +91 9876543210

Available: Mon–Sat, 9 AM to 6 PM

Note: Text queries only. No audio/video support.

You can send your issue summary, application ID (if any), and your village/taluka. Expect a response in 24-48 hours.

📌 Regional Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) Contacts

Find Nearby Centers: kvk.icar.gov.in

Find your local agriculture expert center (KVK) for offline support, free training, guidance on cropping patterns, and field visits.

📢 Tip: Always keep your farmer ID, Aadhaar number, and scheme details handy when calling support for faster assistance. Note down your complaint number if given.
