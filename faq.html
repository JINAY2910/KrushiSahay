Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Krushi Sahay?

It is an all-in-one platform to help farmers access schemes, support, and raise complaints.

2. Is this platform free to use?

Yes, Krushi Sahay is completely free for all farmers.

3. How can I apply for PM-KISAN?

Visit the Government Schemes section and follow the steps under PM-KISAN.

4. Can I raise complaints about scheme delays?

Yes, use our Complaint Portal to raise issues which are forwarded via NGOs to officials.

5. Is there a mobile app for Krushi Sahay?

Currently, we offer a web platform. A mobile app is under development.

6. How do I know if my complaint is received?

After submission, you will receive a confirmation message or call.

7. Can I upload photos as proof?

Yes, the complaint form allows images and PDFs.

8. Are the scheme details updated regularly?

Yes, we keep all scheme data updated as per official announcements.

9. How do I contact Krushi Sahay team?

Use the "Contact Us" page to send a direct message or call.

10. What if I forgot to submit documents?

You can re-upload or visit your local agriculture office for help.

Still have a question? Ask us directly!
